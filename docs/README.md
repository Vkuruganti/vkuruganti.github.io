## About

Vas Kuruganti is an engineer, artificial intelligence systems architect, and site reliability engineering specialist known for his work on large-scale distributed systems, AI infrastructure, and cloud reliability. He has over fifteen years of experience in software engineering and infrastructure architecture, focusing on building reliable and scalable systems for enterprise and cloud environments.
Kuruganti has contributed to areas including site reliability engineering (SRE), AI Development, infrastructure, distributed systems, and cloud platform engineering, with experience designing systems that support machine learning workloads and large-scale data platforms.

Kuruganti studied engineering in India before pursuing graduate education in the United States. He earned a Master of Engineering degree from Boston University, where his academic work focused on complex systems, distributed computing, and artificial intelligence–related disciplines.
His academic background combined electrical engineering and computer systems, which later influenced his work in high-scale infrastructure and machine learning systems.
